Welcome to the Garment Shop

Overview

Learn how garment factories operated in the 1980s. Although taxing and dangerous, factory work empowered workers through community support and Union participation.

How would you like to experience the garment shop?

There are two options.

The first option is to learn in one, continuous story so that you have the choice to stay in one location.

The second option is to explore by physically visiting four locations in the room.
